Host and Hang is a day in which one person (the Host) plans an activity and it is kept a secret to those attending until they arrive at the initial location set by the host. And then you hang (the Hang)! The information you do receive: the date, the approximate time, and the estimated price. We try to keep it budget-friendly and it always involves hanging. You are welcome to invite anyone who you think would enjoy this!
